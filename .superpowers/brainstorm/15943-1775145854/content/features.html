Section 1 — Feature Vector

For each player in the upcoming match, we build this row of inputs for the model

FROM player_recent_form How has this player performed in their last 5 matches?
avg_fantasy_points_last5
Average fantasy pts in last 5 games — strongest signal
avg_runs_last5
Running average of runs scored
avg_wickets_last5
Running average of wickets taken
avg_economy_last5
Recent economy rate (lower = better for bowlers)
FROM player_venue_stats How does this player perform at tonight's ground?
avg_fantasy_points_at_venue
Career fantasy avg at this specific ground
matches_at_venue
Confidence weight — 1 match vs 20 matches is very different
avg_runs_at_venue
Batting avg at this ground (e.g. Rohit at Wankhede)
avg_wickets_at_venue
Bowling avg at this ground
FROM head_to_head_stats How does this player perform against tonight's opposition?
avg_fantasy_points_vs_opp
Career fantasy avg against this specific team
matches_vs_opposition
Confidence weight for h2h stat
avg_runs_vs_opposition
e.g. Kohli averages 55 runs vs MI bowling
avg_wickets_vs_opposition
e.g. Bumrah takes 2 wickets per match vs RCB
